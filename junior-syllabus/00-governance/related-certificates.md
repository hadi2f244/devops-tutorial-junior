# Comprehensive DevOps, Cloud, & Platform Engineering Certification & Literature Guide

This comprehensive guide maps the absolute standard certification pathways, industry-standard learning resources, and definitive reference books directly to each category within modern DevOps and Platform Engineering syllabi.

Use this structure to organize your local `/book` directory (`.pdf` or `.epub`) alongside your main infrastructure and pipeline projects.

---

### 🐧 1. Linux & Host Engineering
*Validates systemd init control, dynamic LVM partitions, file allocation tables (Ext4/XFS), process state lifecycles, permissions, and advanced system optimization.*

#### 🏷️ Target Certifications:
* **RHCSA:** Red Hat Certified System Administrator (Exam **EX200**)
* **RHCE:** Red Hat Certified Engineer (Exam **EX294**)
* **LPIC-1:** Linux Professional Institute Certification - System Administrator (Exams **101-500** and **102-500**)
* **CompTIA Linux+** (Exam **XK0-005**)

#### 📚 Recommended Reference Books:
* **Book 1:** *Red Hat RHCSA 10 Cert Guide: EX200* by Sander van Vugt (Pearson IT Certification)
* **Book 2:** *Linux Bible (10th Edition)* by Christopher Negus (Wiley)
* **Book 3:** *LPIC-1 Linux Professional Institute Certification Study Guide* by Christine Bresnahan and Richard Blum (Sybex)
